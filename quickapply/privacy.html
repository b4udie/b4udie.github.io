Privacy Policy

QuickApply (iOS app, com.b4udie.quickapply) · Effective date: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information QuickApply ("the App", "we") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. QuickApply is an aggregator of IT job listings that helps you discover jobs and prepare and submit job applications.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is needed to provide the App's functionality:

  • Account information. You sign in with Sign in with Apple or Google. We receive and store your email address, your name (if provided by the sign-in provider), and an account identifier issued by that provider. We do not use passwords and never see or store any password of yours.
  • Resume. If you upload a resume (PDF), it is stored on our server so it can be attached to your job applications.
  • Application data. When you create a job application, we store the job listing it refers to, the resume you selected, and any optional cover letter text you write.
  • Search preferences. Job categories, technology tags, work format, and location preferences that you set to filter jobs and receive relevant in-app notifications.

We do not collect analytics or advertising identifiers, do not use third-party tracking SDKs, and do not track you across other apps or websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide the App's features:

  • authenticating you and maintaining your account;
  • storing your resume and preparing job applications on your behalf;
  • matching job listings to your profile and preferences (computed with a deterministic formula on our server);
  • showing you in-app notifications about new job listings that match your preferences.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

3. When Information Is Shared

  • Employers and career sites. The contents of a job application (your resume, cover letter, and related profile details) are transmitted to the relevant employer or its career site only when an application is submitted, and only as a result of your explicit action in the App. We never send your data to employers on our own initiative.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required to do so by law or a valid legal request.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties. Our backend runs on our own infrastructure; we do not pass your data to third-party analytics or marketing services.

4. Job Listings

Job listings shown in the App are aggregated from publicly available career pages of IT companies. Those listings belong to the respective companies. If you follow a link to an employer's website or submit an application, the employer's own privacy policy applies to how they handle your information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a database on our self-hosted server. Data is transmitted between the App and the server over encrypted connections. Access to the server is restricted to the operator of the service.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data (profile, resume, applications, preferences) at any time by contacting us at bratkevychv@proton.me. We will delete your data within a reasonable time after receiving the request.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email address below. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection law (such as the GDPR), which we will honor.

8. Children

QuickApply is not directed to children and is intended for people who are legally able to seek employment. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this page, and the effective date above will be updated when changes are made. Material changes will be communicated in the App.

10. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact: bratkevychv@proton.me
